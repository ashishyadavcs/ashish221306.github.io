यदि reporting verb का object नहीं दिया है तो Secon Person के Pronoun (you ) को First Person में भी बदल देते हैं।

  1. Direct : Ram said ,"You should work hard."
  2. Indirect: Ram told him that he should work hard .

अतः स्पस्ट है की Reporting Verb का object न होने पर Reported Speech के Second Person के Pronoun को आशय तथा सन्दर्भ के अनुसार First Person या Third Person किसी में भी बदल देते हैं।

  1. Direct: He said ,"What have you got in your bag ."
  2. Indirect : He asked me what I had got in my bag .
  1. Direct: He said ,"Why didnot you apply the brake ?"
  2. Indirect: He asked her why she had not applied the brake .
  1. Direct : He said ,"Would you like a drink ?"
  2. Indirect: He asked me if I would linke a drink.

यदि यह स्पष्ट होता है कि you का प्रयोग किसके लिए है तो उसे उसके Person के अनुसार बदल देते हैं। जैसे -

  1. Direct : He said ,"You can go to your house, Anand."
  2. Indirect : He told Anand that he could go to his house.

Rule(3) Direct से Indirect में परिवर्तन करते समय Reported Speech में आएं हुए Third Person के Pronoun ( He , she , it ,they , his ,her , its , their , him , them ) कोई परिवर्तन नहीं करते हैं। जैसे -

  1. Direct : Suresh said ," He is an ordinary clerk."
  2. Indirect : Suresh said that he was an ordiary clerk.
  1. Direct : I said ,"She is not a good player."
  2. Indirect : I said that she was not a good player.

Note (a) : Noun Noun को सदैव Third Person में माना जाता है।
Note (b) : अगर Reporting Verb के subject तथा object दोनों third Person में हों तो Indirect में परिवर्तन करते समय स्पष्टीकरण हेतु () के भीतर उनके सामने उन ब्यक्तिओं का नाम लिख देतें हैं ; जैसे -

  1. Direct : Nirmala said to Meera ,"I shall always help you ."
  2. Indirect : Nirmala told Meera that she (Nirmala) would always help her.

Rules for the change of words expressingnearnessof time or position

Rule 1. Reported Speech में प्रयुक्त समय अथवा स्थिति की निकटता (Nearness of time or position ) सूचक शब्दों को निम्नलिखित प्रकार से बदलते हैं -

Direct Speech Indirect Speech
this that
these those
here there
now then
today that day
tonight that night
last week / year the previous week / year
yesterday the day before
ago before, earlier
the day before yesterday two days before
the day after tomarrow in two day's time

Example

  1. Direct- He said to them ,"I will leave you now."
  2. Indirect - He told them that he would leave them then .
  1. Direct- Mohan said to Ram ," I cannot go with you till next monday."
  2. Indirect -Mohan told Ram that he could not go with him till the following day
  1. Direct- He said to me ," I came here yesterday."
  2. Indirect - He told me that he had gone there the previous day.

Rule 2. यदि this ,here, और now ऐसी किसी वास्तु या स्थान या समय की और संकेत करतें हैं जो कहतें समय वक्ता के सामने उपस्थित हो तो Indirect बनातें समय उनमे कोई परिवर्तन नहीं होता है , बल्कि उन्हें ज्यों का त्यों रख देतें हैं।; eg -

  1. Direct - He said to me ,"This is my house ."
  2. Indirect - He told me that this ( the house before him ) is his house .
  1. Direct - The leader says ," I am glad to be here this morning."
  2. Indirect - The leader says that he is glad to be here this morning (the leader has not left the place).
  1. Direct - Hari said ,"I will do it now ."
  2. Indirect- Hari said that he would do it now ( at the same time ).

Rule 3. this या that का प्रयोग यदि समय की और संकेत के लिए होता है तो Indirect में इनको that या those में ही बदलते हैं। पर यदि this या these का प्रयोग adjective भाति हुआ है तो Indirect बनाते समय That या those के स्थान पर the का प्रयोग भी कर सकते हैं ; eg-

  1. Direct- She sai ," We are going for a picnic this week."
  2. Indirect- She said that they were going for a picnic that week .
  1. Direct- He said ,"I am preparing for a examination these days."
  2. Indirect- He said that he was preparing for the examination those days.
  1. Direct- He said ," I have brought this stick from Nainital."
  2. Indirect- He said that he had brought that stick from Nainital.
  1. Direct- The typist said ."I have typed all these letters."
  2. Indirect- The typist said that he had typed all those letter.

Interrogative Sentence

Interrogative Sentence से प्रश्न का बोध होता है। इस प्रकार के सेंटेंस को Indirect में बदलते समय निम्नलिखित बातों का ध्यान रखना चाहियें -

  1. Reporting verb said या said to को asked या enquired of में बदल देतें हैं।
  2. प्रश्नवाचक वाक्यों में ' that ' Conjunction का प्रयोग नहीं किया जाता है।
    Indirect में प्रश्नवाचक वाक्य को साधारण वाक्य ( Assertive Sentence ) बना देते हैं।
  3. प्रश्नवाचक चिन्ह (?) हटा दिया जाता है और उसके स्थान पर Full stop लगा दिया जाता है।
  4. Reported Speech के Pronoun , Verb तथा अन्य शब्दों को Assertive Sentence के नियमों के अनुसार बदलते है।
  5. जब Direct Speech में ऐसा प्रश्न हो तो जिसका उत्तर yes या ' No ' दिया जा सके अर्थात Reported Speech किसी सहायक क्रिया ( Auxiliary Verb ) जैसे - is, am, are ,was, were, do,does, did, shall, will, has, have ,had, can , may आदि से शुरू हो तो Reported Speech को Connective Word ' If ' या ' Whether' से प्रारम्भ किया जाता हैं ; जैसे -
    Direct Indirect
    Hari said to me, "Are you reading a book?" Hari asked me if I was reading a book.
    Ram said to Hari ,"Will you go to school today?" Ram asked Hari whether he would go to school that day .
    I said to him , " Can you tell me what the time is ?" I asked him if he could tell me what the time was .
  6. अगर Reported Speech में सहायक क्रिया do या does हो और उसके बाद not न हो तो Indirect बनाते समय do/ does को हटा देतें है और मुख्या क्रिया को Past Indefinite Tense में बदल देतें हैं। जैसे -

  1. Direct - She said to reeta , " Can you lend me this watch ?" Reetasaid , "yes".

Imperative Sentence

जब वाकया में Order , Advice या Request की गयी हो तो उसे Imperative Sentence कहतें हैं। इसमें कर्त्ता You छिपा रहता है तथा वाक्य Finite Verb से प्रारम्भ होता है ; जैसे -Go away, Respect your elders , Please give me a pen।

  1. Rule 1. Reporting Verb 'said' को भाव के अनुसार इस प्रकार बदलते हैं -
    1. order (आदेश ) को ordered या commanded में।
    2. Advice (परामर्श ) को - advised , urged आदि में।
    3. Request ( प्रार्थना ) को - requested , begged, implored, entreated आदि में।

  2. Rule 2. Reported speech के verb के पहले To को जोड़कर infinitive बना देते है ; जैसे -
    1. Direct - The teacher said to me , "Sit in your class and learn your lesson."
    2. Indirect - The teacher ordered me to sit in my class and learn my lesson .
    1. Direct - He said to his friend ,"Work hard for the test."
    2. Indirect - He advised his friend to work hard for the test.

  3. Rule 3. Reported speech के Vocative case के noun को Reporting Verb का object बना देतें हैं। जैसे -
    1. Direct - The teacher said to me ,"Sit down boys."
    2. Indirect - The teacher ordered the boys to sit down .
  4. Rule 4. जब Reported Speech , Negative हो अर्थात do not से प्रारम्भ हो तो इसके परिवर्तन की दो विधिया है -

    (a). Reporting Verb को Forbade में बदल दिया जाता है तथा Reported Speech में आये हुए ' do not ' को समाप्त करके क्रिया से पहले to लगा देते हैं ; जैसे -

    1. Direct - The teacher said to the boy , "Do not abuse anyone ."
    2. Indirect - The teacher forbade the boy to abuse anyone .

    5. (b). दूसरी विधि में Reporting Verb , said to को भाव के अनुसार ordered, advised या requested में बदल दिया जाता है। किन्तु इसमें Repported Speech में से ' don't ' के स्थान पर not to का प्रयोग करते हैं।

    1. Direct -The father said to me , "Don't waste your time ."
    2. Indirect - My father advised me not to waste my time .

    Reported Speech जब never से प्रारम्भ हो तो Reporting Verb को advised में बदल देते हैं और never तथा क्रिया के बीच में to का प्रयोग करते हैं। जैसे -

    1. Direct - The teacher said to the student , "Never waste your money ."
    2. Indirect - The teacher advised the sudent never to waste his money.
    never वाले वाक्यों में Forbade का प्रयोग नहीं करते हैं।
  5. rule 6. जब Reported Speech में sir , please, kindly आदि का प्रयोग हो तो Indirect बनाते समय इन शब्दों को हटा देतें है तथा Reporting Verb को Requested में बड़ा देते हैं। जैसे -

    1. Direct - The student said to the teacher , " Sir , mark me present ."
    2. Indirect - The student requested the teacher to mark him present .

    1. Direct - Ram said to shyam , "Please come and tell me something about the match ."
    2. Indirect - Ram requested Shyam to come and tell hm something about the match .
